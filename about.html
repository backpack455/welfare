About Us

Welcome to todocode Established Since 2019

In 2019, our three co-founders met up and realized that kids in the metro Detroit area were not being exposed to coding. todocode was founded out of that realization, and since then, todocode has started a summer program for kids to learn coding.

We operate primarily in physical classes but due to current pandemic most kids are staying at home during the coronavirus outbreak, todocode has created an online class. Join it today!
